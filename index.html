Instructions

  • You control the red car on the left track
  • Other cars will move on the other track. Eventually the number of cars will increase.
  • Your objective is save your car from colliding with other cars and achieve greatest score(Laps) possibe.
  • Your controls for Desktop:
    • UP ARROW : Accelerate
    • DOWN ARROW : Declarate
    • R : Reset/Restart game
  • Press UP ARROW/green to start.
